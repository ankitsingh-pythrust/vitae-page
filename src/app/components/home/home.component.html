About VitaePage

VitaePage replaces static resumes with a personal career site that looks great on any device and makes your achievements easy to scan. It's built for people who want a professional presence without learning web design.

  • Built for students, engineers, designers, marketers, and freelancers
  • Privacy-first: keep drafts private, share securely, or go public
  • Performance-focused: fast load times and accessible defaults

How It Works

1. Import
Connect LinkedIn or upload your PDF. We parse roles, education, skills, and links into structured sections.
2. Choose a template
Pick a clean, recruiter-friendly layout with adjustable colors and section order.
3. Refine
Add metrics, featured projects, certifications, and custom sections like Awards or Volunteering.
4. Publish
Go live at a vitae.page link or your custom domain. Enable analytics and SEO in one click.

Frequently Asked Questions

No. Everything is guided. Pick a template, reorder sections, and publish in clicks.
Can I use my own domain?

Yes. Pro and Career include custom domains with automatic SSL.
Is my data private?

You control visibility by page: private draft, password-protected, or public. Export or delete data anytime.
